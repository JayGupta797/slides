We treat sleep like a budget line we can overdraw indefinitely. The body keeps a stricter ledger. Below is a portrait of when, on average, the country is unconscious — drawn from time-use diaries kept by tens of thousands of Americans — paired with what we know happens when you cheat the curve, and a few small habits, anchored to the clock.

Fig. 02 · The arithmetic of a short night

What an hour costs.

Sleep loss is not linear. The fifth hour you skip costs more than the first. Below: what one week of partial nights does to a body, by recent measure.

A day, arranged for sleep.

Sleep is mostly built during the day. The eight habits below are ordered by clock, not importance — anchor as many as you can. The first three matter most.

  1. Within 30 min of waking01

    Walk into the light.

    10 minutes of outdoor light, even overcast, anchors the circadian clock and sets melatonin release tonight.

  2. +1 hr · breakfast02

    Eat early, eat protein.

    A daytime feeding window (roughly 7a–7p) reinforces the same clock light started. Late eating fragments deep sleep.

  3. Before 2 p.m.03

    Last coffee of the day.

    Caffeine has a 5–7 hour half-life. A 4 p.m. cup is still working at 11 p.m., even if you fall asleep — you'll get less of stage 3.

  4. Late afternoon04

    Move, but not too late.

    30 minutes of exercise deepens slow-wave sleep — given 3 hours before bed, core temperature falls.

  5. 3 hr before bed05

    Close the bar.

    Alcohol sedates but it does not sleep you. It suppresses REM and fragments the second half of the night.

  6. 1 hr before bed06

    Dim the room by half.

    Bright overhead light at 10 p.m. delays melatonin by ~90 minutes. Lamps and warm bulbs do not. The fix is cheap.

  7. Bedtime · every night07

    Same time, every time.

    Regularity predicts mortality better than duration. A wobble of 90 minutes nightly tracks worse than sleeping less.

  8. Cannot sleep?08

    Leave the bed.

    If you've been awake 20 minutes, get up. Read, dimly, somewhere else. The bed should stay associated with sleep.
