About this survey

This is a topline report — the frequency of every response, exactly as collected, with no inferential analysis. Every question was optional, so the denominator (N) varies between questions. Questions marked "check all that apply" may sum to more than 100%. Percentages are rounded.

N = 331 members of the Class of 2029, surveyed September 2025.

Methodology

Self-reported. Anonymous. Optional. Counted.
